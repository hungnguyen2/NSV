NatSteelVina Ltd. Company is the Joint Venture of NatSteel Group (NatSteel Holdings now) which is one of the leading Industry Group in Singapore, and Vietnam Steel Corporation. It was established on the basis of 711/CP Investment License dated on November 2th, 1993. It was built in Thai Nguyen City, Thai Nguyen Province.

NatSteelVina inherits and promotes the strength of both Joint Venture partners in researching and developing applications. The company has an experienced Management Team, enthusiastic and dedicated and highly-qualified staff, and modern production line system imported from Italy. It also applies synchronization in information management system, inputs, sales and customer care services.

NatSteel is reaching the goal of providing the best quality products, non-stop product quality improvements. Besides, NatSteel is evaluated as the steel company with the best service in Vietnam.

NatSteel is a leading steel producer in Asia Pacific. The NatSteel group has an annual combined steel production capacity of over 2 million tonnes, with operations across Australia, China, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand and Vietnam.

NatSteel delivers premium steel products and solutions for the construction industry, including upstream products as well as customised downstream products and services. Its products are used in residential, industrial and commercial projects, as well as infrastructural works.

Singapore is home to NatSteel’s headquarters as well as its flagship plant. NatSteel is the only steel mill in Singapore with an integrated upstream and downstream operations, where steel is manufactured through recycling scrap, and also fabricated according to customers’ needs. In fact, NatSteel has one of the largest single cut-and-bend operations in the world.

As part of Tata Steel – a top global steel company –  NatSteel is poised for further growth, and aspires to be the global steel industry benchmark for value creation and corporate citizenship.
